Video Creator and Editor (Unpaid Intern Position)

Location: Remote

Description:

We are looking for a creative and talented Video Creator and Editor to join our team. This position involves creating engaging videos for both YouTube and YouTube shorts. As an unpaid position, we understand it limits our options, but we are willing to offer some benefits to support your work. We will pay for software subscriptions, image licensing, and other necessary resources, ensuring it costs nothing out of your pocket. Additionally, you'll have the opportunity to earn up to 75% of the video's ad revenue and up to 25% of any affiliate or sponsorship revenue directly attributed to the video's success.

Responsibilities:

  • Produce high-quality and captivating videos for YouTube and YouTube shorts
  • Edit videos to match the tone and style of our brand
  • Collaborate with the content team to brainstorm and execute video ideas
  • Optimize videos for different platforms and formats (horizontal and vertical video editing)
  • Stay updated with industry trends and best practices in video creation and editing

Requirements:

  • Proficiency in video editing software (e.g., Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, DaVinci Resolve)
  • Creativity and a keen eye for visual storytelling
  • Familiarity with YouTube content creation best practices
  • Ability to meet deadlines and work independently
  • Strong communication and teamwork skills

How to Apply:

If you are passionate about creating compelling videos and excited about the opportunity to grow with us, please apply through our Contact page. Include brief message explaining why you are interested in this position.

We look forward to hearing from you and exploring the world of video creation together!

Social Media Manager (Unpaid Intern Position)

Location: Remote

Description:

We are seeking a motivated and enthusiastic Social Media Manager to join our team. As a Social Media Manager, you will be responsible for some of our organization's social media presence across various platforms. While this is an unpaid position, we want to provide you with valuable opportunities to grow your skills and showcase your expertise in social media management.

Responsibilities:

  • Create and curate engaging content for our social media channels
  • Develop and implement social media strategies to increase brand awareness and reach
  • Monitor and respond to comments, messages, and mentions
  • Analyze social media metrics and adjust strategies accordingly
  • Collaborate with the content team to align social media efforts with overall marketing goals

Requirements:

  • Proven experience in managing social media accounts (personal or professional)
  • Familiarity with social media platforms and their respective best practices
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to think creatively and generate engaging content ideas
  • Passion for social media and staying up-to-date with industry trends

How to Apply:

If you are passionate about social media and excited about the opportunity to gain practical experience, please apply through our Contact page. Include brief message explaining why you are interested in this position.

We look forward to receiving your application and embarking on a rewarding social media journey together!
